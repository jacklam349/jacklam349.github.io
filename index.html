Trekker Hassan

Is a tour guide licensed by the the Royaume Du Maroc, Ministry of Tourism and has over 15 years of experience planning for and accompanying visitors to Morocco on a wide variety fun and informative tours. He enjoys sharing his Berber culture, including cuisine, music, economics, politics, historic sites, architecture and more; ranging from the stunningly beautiful High Atlas Mountains to the great silent expanses of the Sahara. He is independent of formal agencies, but will work with them, if it is the best thing for his guest. So he is able to arrange for you tailor made visit, considering your specific interests, budget and physical abilities.
